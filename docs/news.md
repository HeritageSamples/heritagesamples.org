# News & Updates

This page provides brief updates on the development of the Heritage Samples Registry, including milestones, pilot activities, community engagement, and future plans.

Updates are listed in reverse chronological order, with the most recent items first.

---

## 2026-02-24 — Initial website launch (draft)

The first draft of the Heritage Samples Registry website has been released.  
This initial version provides an overview of the Registry, explains the role of IGSNs, and outlines the planned roadmap and consortium vision.

Work will now focus on pilot testing, community engagement, and refining guidance for institutions interested in participating.
